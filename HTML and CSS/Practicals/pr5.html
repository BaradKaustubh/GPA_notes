GOVERMENT POLYTECHNIC AMRAVATI

Government Polytechnic, Amravati is an autonomous Institute of Govt. Polytechnic, Maharashtra established in the year 1955. This institute has a long history of producing technical manpower and rendering technical services to the society. Many students of this institute are chairing the topmost positions in various govt. offices and in reputed industries.
