ALL ABOUT MY ALMA MATER




It is said that reminiscence of the past should be done as long as it gives pleasure and happiness. And this is what exactly happens with me when I remember my Alma Mater…nurturer, my haven, a place I called my own for 5 years. The school has the most important role to play in shaping one’s personality. As far as I am concerned I can very humbly say, I am what I am because of my schooling.


I am very grateful to my parents for having chosen this institution, which even after 65 years of its existence still stands rock-solid to its motto, ‘Truth and Love’. My heart is filled with pride, fondness, gratitude-coupled with nostalgia and longing as to how those wonderful years flew by.
